Phase One
Days 0 – 30

Launch & System Activation.

Your infrastructure is built. Now the two core acquisition systems begin to activate. This first phase is about initialization — establishing the foundation that makes Month 2 and beyond perform. Volume is intentionally conservative at this stage. That is by design.

Core System 01  ·  Shared Infrastructure
Intelligent Client
Acquisition System (ICAS)
Active from Day 1

ICAS is ScaleLogix's proprietary AI-powered outreach and client acquisition engine — a shared infrastructure designed to support new agency partners during the critical early launch phase. While your dedicated systems come online, ICAS introduces qualified opportunities sourced from ScaleLogix's own outbound channels directly into your pipeline.

ICAS represents the operational bridge between your launch date and full independence. It is a launch accelerator, not a long-term primary channel — and it is designed specifically to prevent the quiet first 30–45 days that agencies without this infrastructure typically experience.

How ICAS Works
Shared outbound channels generate early deal flow. Active source channels may include LinkedIn outreach, cold email campaigns, Facebook lead generation, marketplace platforms such as Upwork, and additional channels as they are developed and tested. Channel availability varies based on market conditions and operational capacity.
ScaleLogix's internal team handles initial qualification. Inbound interest generated through ICAS channels is processed by ScaleLogix's B2B operations team, which conducts initial qualification conversations where applicable. Prospects that meet criteria are then routed to agency partners based on niche alignment and availability.
Early outreach starts light and builds over time. ICAS outreach is intentionally conservative during system warm-up. Aggressive early output degrades deliverability and platform standing — patience in this phase produces compounding returns.
Supporting resources are included. ICAS comes with sales frameworks and discovery guidance, proposal and pricing references, and diagnostic tools used in prospect conversations — resources designed to help you convert opportunities effectively from the start.
ICAS is a launch accelerator. It is not designed to be your primary long-term acquisition channel. As your agency scales, you are expected to operate and invest in your own marketing and outreach infrastructure — which is exactly what the APEX Lead Engine is built to power.
Core System 02  ·  Your Dedicated Infrastructure
APEX Lead Engine
Activates Month 1–2

The APEX Lead Engine is your agency's own dedicated client acquisition infrastructure — built and operated entirely within your CRM environment. Where ICAS draws from ScaleLogix's shared channels, APEX generates opportunities specifically for your business. It is the foundation of your independent, long-term acquisition strategy.

ICAS vs. APEX
ICAS distributes from shared ScaleLogix channels. APEX runs inside your own CRM and generates opportunities exclusively for your agency. They are complementary — not competing.
What APEX Does
Targeted outbound sourcing, automated email sequences, AI-assisted response handling, and CRM-based follow-up workflows — all working to convert engagement into booked discovery calls for your team.
What's Included in APEX
Targeted outbound lead sourcing. Lists are researched and curated based on your niche, positioning, and ideal client profile.
Automated email outreach sequences. Multi-step, personalized outreach campaigns deployed from within your infrastructure — running continuously in the background.
AI-assisted response handling. As prospects engage with outreach, the system responds intelligently, nurtures conversations, and moves interested leads toward a booked call — without requiring manual input on every exchange.
CRM-based follow-up workflows. Structured, automated follow-up sequences ensure no opportunity falls through the cracks — from initial engagement to booked discovery call.
APEX is actively enhanced on an ongoing basis. Upcoming developments include expanded outbound channels, automated prospect qualification, and integrated calling workflows for follow-up on active leads. As your agency scales, most partners layer additional channels — paid acquisition, LinkedIn, partnerships, and niche-specific outbound — on top of the APEX foundation.
Why Activating an Acquisition Channel in Month 1 Matters
ICAS runs in the background from Day 1. But partners who activate at least one additional acquisition channel — LinkedIn Outreach or a Lead Acquisition package — in Month 1 consistently see faster deal flow, shorter ramp periods, and clearer market feedback. This is not required. But it significantly closes the window of a quiet early phase. The optional programs described in Phase Two are available to activate at any time.

Optional Program 01
LinkedIn Outreach

Professional LinkedIn outreach deployed and fully managed by ScaleLogix. You choose how it is positioned and which approach fits your business — we handle all setup, sequencing, audience targeting, and ongoing management. Three configurations are available depending on your goals and preferences.

Dedicated Outreach Profiles
ScaleLogix deploys and manages dedicated outreach profiles on your behalf — fully managed, completely separate from your personal brand. Capacity scales based on your program scope. Ideal if you prefer separation or want outreach running independently of your personal presence.
