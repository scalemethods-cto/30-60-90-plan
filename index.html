Once You're Live

ICAS activates immediately upon launch. Your CRM begins receiving inbound and outbound data signals within the first week. Cold outreach sequences, including in-house LinkedIn, email, and direct outreach, begin routing to your agency. Responses and engagement are tagged and funneled directly into your pipeline.

Early Volume Is Conservative by Design

Cold outreach starts intentionally light. It is algorithmically designed to round-robin based on niche and tier. This phase focuses on warming domains, profiles, and audiences. Expect signals and conversations, not heavy volume yet.

Why We Recommend Starting Acquisition in Month 1

While ICAS runs continuously in the background, we strongly recommend activating at least one acquisition channel in Month 1. This is not required, but in practice, clients who begin acquisition early consistently outperform those who wait.

Month 1–2
Acquisition Options & Ramp-Up
Choose your channels. Build momentum.

We'll help you choose the most appropriate starting option based on your niche and goals. Most partners begin with one or both of the following channels.

Channel 01

LinkedIn Campaigns

  • Run using your own profile(s) or a network of profiles under your brand
  • Typical ramp once live: ~6–12 qualified conversations per profile per month
  • Scalable with additional profiles under your brand
  • Monthly fee applies per profile if expanded

If you plan to use LinkedIn, let us know early so setup can begin before or immediately after launch.

Channel 02

Lead Packages

  • Delivered via ScaleLogix-managed funnels
  • Pricing is per lead or per booked meeting
  • Costs fluctuate based on niche, market competition, and qualification criteria
  • Setup typically takes 10–15 business days (creative alignment, funnel configuration, CRM routing)
Typical starting budget: $700–$2,500 / Month 1
